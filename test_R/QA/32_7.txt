Q. stochastic gradient descent is
A. more likely to get stuck in local minima
B. less likely to get stuck in global minima
C. none of the above
D. less likely to get stuck in local minima
(D)

Q. Batch gradient descent is
A. less likely to get stuck in local minima
B. more likely to get stuck in local minima
C. more likely to get stuck in global minima
D. none of the above
(B)
